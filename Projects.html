Projects

1.Investigation on the interfacial shear mechanism of Steel reinforced concrete composite bridge

Principal Investigator Sep. 2018-Present
•   Conducted push-out tests on SRC specimens with or without connectors.
•   Performed numerical research by using ABAQUS to further reveal the shear transferring mechanism at the steel-concrete interface.
•   Published one research papers (First author, SCI Q1) and two conference papers.

2.Investigation on the static performance of pier-girder composite joint for composite rigid frame bridge

Principal Investigator Dec. 2017-Present
•   Propesed a novel composite joint with PBL connectors for composite rigid frame bridge.
•   Performed numerical research by adopting ANSYS to investigate the load transfer mechanism between steel girder and concrete pier.
•   Conducted theoretical study on such joint and derived simplified calculation formula.
•   Carried out numerical simulation on the structural performance of the whole bridge by means of ANSYS and wrote calculation report.
•   Published one utility model patent.

Principal Investigator Apr. 2019-Present
